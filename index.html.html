Mia's Amazing Banana Bread

Here I will tell you my special recipe for my amazing banana bread. It has been passed on for many generations.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mash the ripe bananas.
  3. Mix in melted butter.
  4. Add baking soda and salt.
  5. Mix in sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  6. Add flour and stir.
  7. Pour into loaf pan and bake.

I hope you enjoy making and eating this banana bread as much as I do!
